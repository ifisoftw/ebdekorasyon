# EB Dekorasyon - Profesyonel Görsel Prompts

Bu dosya, EB Dekorasyon hizmetleri için gerçekçi, dönüşüm odaklı ve Türkiye ev tipolojisine uygun görsel oluşturmak amacıyla hazırlanmış promptları içerir.

> **Genel Stil Notu:** Tüm görseller fotorealistik (8k, hyper-realistic), profesyonel iç mekan fotoğrafçılığı (architectural photography) tarzında, doğal ışık alan, temiz ve düzenli mekanları yansıtmalıdır. "Before/After" konseptleri için split-screen (bölünmüş ekran) kompozisyonlar önerilir.

---

## 1. Mobilya Boya ve Renk Değişimi Kategorisi

### Service 1: Mutfak Dolabı Boyama ve Renk Değişimi
**Konsept:** Eski kahverengi mutfağın, modern mat beyaz/griye dönüşümü.
**Prompt:**
> Split screen comparison, realistic interior photography. **Left side (Before):** Dated, dark brown wooden kitchen cabinets in a typical Turkish apartment, slightly yellow harsh lighting, old-fashioned handles. **Right side (After):** The exact same kitchen layout but transformed into a modern masterpiece. Cabinets painted in matte "Bianca" white and soft "Anthracite" grey. Modern led under-cabinet lighting, sleek black metal handles, bright daylight, clean white quartz countertop. High resolution, architectural digest style, wide angle shot.

### Service 2: Mobilya ve Ahşap Kapı Boyama
**Konsept:** Sararmış Amerikan kapının bembeyaz ve pürüzsüz hale gelişi.
**Prompt:**
> Split screen comparison of an interior door. **Left side:** An old, yellowed American panel door with scratches, dim hallway lighting, looking worn out. **Right side:** The same door freshly painted in pristine bright white, smooth satin finish (lake boya), sparkling chrome door handle, bright and clean hallway with light parquet floor. Professional close-up shot emphasizing the smooth paint texture, 8k resolution.

### Service 3: Sandalye ve Masa Boyama
**Konsept:** Klasik bir yemek masasının modern "Country" tarzına yenilenmesi.
**Prompt:**
> A beautiful dining room transformation. Center focus on a refurbished wooden dining table and chairs. The table top is natural walnut wood finish with a rich glossy varnish, while the table legs and chairs are painted in matte cream white (country style). Soft natural sunlight streaming through tulle curtains (tül perde), Turkish home setting, a vase with fresh flowers on the table. High detailed texture of the wood and paint, photorealistic.

### Service 4: Döşeme Yüzü Değişimi
**Konsept:** Eskimiş berjerin lüks kadife kumaşla kaplanması.
**Prompt:**
> Professional upholstery workshop shot. In the foreground, a luxurious armchair (Berjer) newly upholstered in deep emerald green velvet fabric with gold button tufting (Chesterfield style). Next to it, fabric swatches (kartela) spread out. In the blurred background, a craftsman working on another furniture piece. Warm lighting, emphasizing the high-quality texture and rich color of the new fabric.

### Service 5: Yatak Odası Mobilya Boyama
**Konsept:** Koyu renk yatak odası takımının modern griye dönüşümü.
**Prompt:**
> A serene, renovated bedroom using "Before and After" style. **Left:** A heavy, dark mahogany wardrobe and nightstand making the room look small and gloomy. **Right:** The same furniture painted in a soft, matte "Dove Grey" color, making the room look spacious and airy. Modern bedding, soft ambient lighting, clean lines. 8k resolution, interior design magazine quality.

## 2. Tamirat, Tadilat ve Dekorasyon Kategorisi

### Service 6: Mutfak Tezgahı Değişimi ve Tamiri
**Konsept:** Çizik eski tezgahın yerini pırıl pırıl Calacatta mermer görünümlü bir tezgaha bırakması.
**Prompt:**
> Close-up detail shot of a kitchen countertop installation. **Focus:** A pristine, white quartz countertop with subtle grey veins (Calacatta Gold style) being installed. A stainless steel undermount sink and a modern chrome faucet. On the counter, a bowl of lemons for color contrast. Background shows modern white cabinets. Bright, clean, hygiene-focused, hyper-realistic architectural photography.

### Service 7: TV Ünitesi ve Duvar Dekorasyonu
**Konsept:** Duvar çıtalama ve modern TV ünitesi ile şık bir salon duvarı.
**Prompt:**
> A luxurious living room wall design. A large flat-screen TV mounted on a wall featuring elegant decorative molding (duvar çıtalama) painted in soft beige. Integrated LED strip lighting behind the TV panel creates a warm glow. Below the TV, a floating modern console unit in walnut wood. A cozy, modern Turkish salon atmosphere with a plush sofa corner visible. 8k, cinematic lighting.

### Service 8: Kafe İçi Dekorasyon ve Tasarım
**Konsept:** Sıcak, davetkar, "Instagrammable" bir kafe iç mekanı.
**Prompt:**
> Wide angle shot of a trendy, newly designed coffee shop in Istanbul. Industrial-chic style with brick walls, Edison bulb pendant lights, and custom-made wooden tables. A velvet green seating bench (sedir) runs along the wall. The atmosphere is warm and inviting, with a professional barista counter in the background. High detail, 8k resolution, photorealistic interior design render vibe.

### Service 9: Mobilya Tamiratı ve Bakımı
**Konsept:** Usta bir elin menteşe/ray tamiri yapması, güven verici detay.
**Prompt:**
> Close-up action shot of a professional handyman's hands repairing a cabinet hinge. Using a modern cordless drill, fixing a "broken" sliding wardrobe door rail. Focus on the tool and the mechanism. The background is a clean, blurry domestic interior. Lighting emphasizes precision and expertise. High resolution, sharp focus.

### Service 10: Ev İçi Genel Dekorasyon
**Konsept:** Anahtar teslim tadilat sonrası tertemiz, boş modern bir daire.
**Prompt:**
> A stunning, fully renovated empty apartment living room ready for move-in. Shinny laminate parquet flooring, freshly painted walls in "satin white", sophisticated suspended ceiling with hidden LED cove lighting. Large windows with city view of Istanbul. The space looks spacious, clean, and high-end. Architectural real estate photography style, wide angle.

## 3. Zemin ve Parke Sistemleri Kategorisi

### Service 11: Laminant Parke Döşeme
**Konsept:** Zemin döşeme ustasının iş başında olması ve parkenin kalitesi.
**Prompt:**
> Low angle shot of a skilled worker installing huge planks of light oak laminate flooring. The floor looks seamless, warm, and natural. Beside the worker, a stack of high-quality parquet boxes (brand visible like AGT or Çamsan style). Soft natural light hitting the floor texture, highlighting the wood grain. Professional construction photography.

### Service 12: Parke Kurulumu ve Renk Çeşitleri
**Konsept:** Balıksırtı (Herringbone) döşemenin estetik duruşu.
**Prompt:**
> Top-down or high-angle view of a floor being laid with "Herringbone" (Balıksırtı) pattern parquet in a rich medium-oak color. The geometric pattern is perfect and mesmerizing. The transition to a white wall with a tall white skirting board (süpürgelik) is visible. Elegant, sophisticated interior design detail shot, photorealistic, 8k.
